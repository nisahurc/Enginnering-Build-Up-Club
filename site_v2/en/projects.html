1.

Robot Vacuum

Objective of the Project:

The design of the robot vacuum cleaner was created in a completely original way. Plexiglass was used as the material, and a gas laser machine was utilized for cutting. The electronic connections and embedded system software were also uniquely written and made operational. For the optimized and advanced version, much more sophisticated features such as mapping and smart charging were considered. However, due to economic constraints, the project has been put on hold.

Aim of the Project:

The purpose of the project is to provide the acquisition of basic-level electronic, mechanical, and software skills and to aim for the development of self-confidence for future projects.

2.

RC Tank

Our vehicle serves as a preliminary prototype for the electric vehicle we aim to develop in the long term. In its electronic connections, an electronic board design has been created using the EagleCAD application, ensuring that the connections are organized on the circuit board. While the vehicle will be in motion, it will also communicate with the station through a telemetry system.

3.

Electrical Scooter

Our Electric Scooter project is an initiative carried out under the Seyhan Technology and Innovation Center. The primary goal is to develop a walking scooter in the field of intelligent transportation systems. After a month-long process, the chassis was completed, and components such as the motor, battery, and ESC were installed, resulting in the final version.
